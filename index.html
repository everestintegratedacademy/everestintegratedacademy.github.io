A message from the Principal.

Welcome to Everest Integrated Academy. We are very excited to know that you have thought about giving our school a choice to instill your child(ren) with wisdom, understanding and knowledge. At Everest, we ensure and try our very best to adhere to our vision and mission. Through this motivation, we drive ourselves to bring out the best in every student. At Everest, we use the Accelerated Christian Education (A.C.E) curriculum provided to us by Accelerated Education Enterprises (AEE). This curriculum enables us to give your child(ren) Biblical knowledge and teach them to practice Godly wisdom. Through this way, we have enabled and helped many of our students to lead successful lives in a broken world. We continue to hope that Everest Integrated Academy will be the ideal school for your family and loved ones. Be Blessed!


Kyra Wess - Principal & Founder
