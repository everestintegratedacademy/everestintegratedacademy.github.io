Payment Plans

  • Annual Payment: Full payment due by August 1st (5% discount applied)
  • Semester Payment: Two payments due by August 1st and January 1st
  • Monthly Payment: Ten monthly payments from August to May

Payment Methods

We accept the following payment methods:

  • Credit/Debit Card
  • Bank Transfer
  • Check
  • 529 College Savings Plan

Financial Aid

Everest Integrated Academy is committed to making quality education accessible. We offer various financial aid options to eligible families:

  • Need-based scholarships
  • Merit-based scholarships
  • Sibling discounts
  • Employee discounts

Our financial aid program aims to bridge the gap between the cost of education and what a family can afford. We consider various factors, including income, assets, and special circumstances.

Scholarships

Everest Integrated Academy offers several scholarship opportunities to recognize and support outstanding students:

  • Academic Excellence Scholarship
  • Leadership and Community Service Award
  • STEM Achievement Scholarship
  • Arts and Creativity Grant
  • Athletic Performance Scholarship

Scholarship applications are accepted annually. Please check our scholarship portal for deadlines and specific requirements.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in the tuition fees?

Our tuition fees cover instruction, textbooks, standard school supplies, and access to our facilities and technology resources.

Are there any additional fees?

Some extracurricular activities, field trips, and specialized programs may have additional fees. These will be communicated in advance.

How do I apply for financial aid?

You can apply for financial aid by clicking the "Apply for Financial Aid" button on our Financial Aid tab. The application process typically opens in January for the following academic year.

What is the BEYOND Program?

The BEYOND Program is our advanced curriculum designed for gifted students. It offers accelerated learning opportunities, specialized projects, and enhanced resources to challenge and inspire high-achieving students.
