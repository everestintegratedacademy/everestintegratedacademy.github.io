PEAK Curriculum

Personalized Education and Academic Knowledge

Our Mission

To provide a transformative educational experience that empowers students to reach their full potential academically, spiritually, and personally, preparing them to be leaders and innovators in a rapidly changing world.

Our Vision

To be the premier Christian educational institution, recognized globally for our innovative curriculum, holistic approach to student development, and commitment to producing graduates who make a positive impact on society.

ELEVATING EDUCATION TO NEW HEIGHTS

The PEAK curriculum is Everest Integrated Academy's pioneering educational framework, officially launched on November 27, 2023. This date marks the beginning of a new era in education, where innovation, tradition, and holistic development converge to shape the future of learning. Inspired by the academy’s commitment to creating leaders and visionaries, PEAK is designed to not only challenge the mind but also cultivate the spirit and soul.


Rooted in Christian values and driven by a vision for the future, PEAK was conceived after years of research, exploration, and collaboration among educators, spiritual leaders, and innovators. The academy’s founders aimed to address the growing complexities of the modern world, where both technological advancement and moral leadership are critical. Thus, PEAK was born—not just as a curriculum but as a transformative educational experience that equips students to face the challenges and opportunities of the 21st century.


At its core, PEAK emphasizes that education should be as dynamic as the world in which we live. Its unveiling on that brisk November morning symbolized more than just a curriculum; it celebrated innovation and faith. The PEAK model represents a new approach to learning, merging academic rigor with personalized, hands-on experiences and a strong ethical foundation.


PEAK’s innovative approach seamlessly integrates STEM disciplines with the humanities, cultivating not just critical thinkers but also creators, problem solvers, and future leaders. Each aspect of the curriculum is designed to challenge students intellectually, while nurturing their emotional intelligence and spiritual well-being. The Christian principles that guide Everest Integrated Academy ensure that students develop a strong moral compass, a deep sense of empathy, and a commitment to servant leadership.


PEAK’s pillars—such as Academic Excellence, Critical Thinking, Leadership, Spiritual Growth, and Global Citizenship—are not merely abstract concepts but living principles. These elements are experienced through leadership projects, entrepreneurship opportunities, and global exchange programs. One moving story from PEAK's inaugural year involved middle school students developing a sustainable energy initiative for underprivileged communities—an example of how students apply their learning to make a real-world impact.


The curriculum places great emphasis on personalized learning. Students are encouraged to pursue their unique talents through advanced coursework, entrepreneurship, and STEM electives, including cutting-edge subjects like Robotics and AI. This blend of advanced academics and personal development empowers students to explore their passions and excel.


Recognizing the importance of digital literacy, PEAK provides students with essential technology skills and a strong foundation in digital ethics. Courses in data science, coding, and more ensure that students are well-prepared for the technological landscape of tomorrow, equipped with both the skills and the moral guidance needed to lead responsibly.


The unveiling of PEAK on November 27, 2023 was more than a milestone for Everest Integrated Academy—it was a turning point in education, representing a bold new standard in holistic, faith-based, and forward-thinking instruction. With PEAK, students are prepared not only for academic success but to make a lasting, positive impact on the world as compassionate, driven, and ethical leaders.

The Nine Pillars of PEAK

Academic Excellence

Rigorous coursework in core subjects to build a strong foundation, with advanced options for accelerated learners.

Critical Thinking & Innovation

Developing analytical skills, problem-solving abilities, and fostering creativity across all disciplines.

Character & Leadership

Instilling values, ethics, and leadership skills through community service and mentorship programs.

Global Citizenship

Cultivating awareness and responsibility in an interconnected world through cultural studies and exchange programs.

Spiritual Growth

Nurturing faith and Christian values through biblical studies, worship, and faith-based community activities.

Digital Literacy

Equipping students with essential technological skills and understanding of digital ethics for the modern world.

Holistic Well-being

Promoting physical, mental, and emotional health through sports, counseling, and mindfulness practices.

Creative Expression

Encouraging artistic development and self-expression through various forms of art, music, and performance.

Environmental Stewardship

Fostering a deep understanding of environmental issues and promoting sustainable practices.

    PEAK Journey Map

    Elementary (Grades 1-5)

    Foundation building years focusing on core subjects, critical thinking, and character development. Students engage in hands-on learning experiences and begin to explore their interests through various enrichment activities.

    • Core subject mastery
    • Introduction to STEM concepts
    • Character education
    • Arts and music integration

    Junior (Grades 6-8)

    Transition years emphasizing academic rigor, personal responsibility, and leadership skills. Students are introduced to more advanced concepts and begin to specialize in areas of interest.

    • Advanced math and science
    • Critical writing and research skills
    • Introduction to foreign languages
    • Leadership and community service

    Middle (Grades 9-10)

    Preparatory years for advanced studies, focusing on depth of knowledge, research skills, and community engagement. Students begin to shape their academic and career paths through elective choices and extracurricular activities.

    • Specialized STEM courses
    • Advanced humanities and social sciences
    • Career exploration
    • Entrepreneurship projects

    High (Grades 11-13)

    Advanced studies preparing students for higher education and beyond. Rigorous coursework, including AP and IB options, internships, and capstone projects help students develop college-ready skills and explore potential career paths.

    • College-level courses and research
    • Internship opportunities
    • Global exchange programs
    • College and career counseling

    BEYOND Program

    Post-graduate opportunities for exceptional students, including advanced research projects, university-level courses, and global leadership programs. The BEYOND program bridges the gap between high school and higher education or career entry.

    • University partnerships
    • Research fellowships
    • Global leadership initiatives
    • Entrepreneurship incubator
